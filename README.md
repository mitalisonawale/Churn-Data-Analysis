## 📉 Customer Churn Data Analysis  
**Data Analytics Project — Customer Behavior & Retention Insights**

---

### 📌 Problem Statement  
Customer churn is a critical challenge for subscription-based businesses. This project analyzes user behavior, engagement patterns, and subscription data to identify factors influencing churn and provide actionable insights to improve customer retention.

---

### 📊 Dataset Overview  
- **Churn Analysis Based On:** Gender, Engagement, Subscription, Watch Behavior  
- **Key Metrics:** Watch Time, Inactive Days, Videos Watched, Subscription Period  
- **User Behavior Tracked:** Daily usage, Night activity, Customer support interaction

### 🚦 Key Insights  

- ✔ **Female users** have slightly higher churn rate than male users  
- ✔ Users with **multi-screen access** show significantly higher churn risk  
- ✔ **Average age (~39)** is similar for churned and retained users  
- ✔ **Higher weekly watch time** is linked to higher churn (binge behavior)  
- ✔ **Email subscription reduces churn** significantly  
- ✔ Users with **~3 inactive days/week** are more likely to churn  
- ✔ **Low engagement users** have higher churn probability  
- ✔ **Consistent watching behavior reduces churn risk**  
- ✔ **Binge users (high daily watch time)** tend to churn after consuming content  

---

### 💡 Business Recommendations  

To reduce churn and improve retention:  
- 🔹 Promote **email subscriptions** to keep users engaged  
- 🔹 Target **high-risk users (multi-screen users)** with retention offers  
- 🔹 Reduce churn by improving **content recommendation systems**  
- 🔹 Encourage **consistent engagement** rather than binge consumption  
- 🔹 Re-engage inactive users with **notifications and offers**  
- 🔹 Monitor users with **high watch time** for potential churn risk  

---

### 🌍 Business Impact (Simulation)  

If churn is reduced by **5%**:  
- 🔸 Increased customer retention and lifetime value  
- 🔸 Higher subscription revenue  
- 🔸 Improved user engagement and platform growth
